About Me

My name is Roberto Escobar, from San Francisco California but originally from Peru, i come from from an immigrant family, parents arrived in the United States in the late 90's looking for better opportunities as many other families do. I arrived in August of 2001, coming here as a 10 year old kid was a lot for me to handle at times, i would feel out of place and there was the language barrier, i didn't know how to speak english at all. Thankfully i had a great support system at school and at home and that helped me get through all the tough times i had.
I'd describe myself as a hardworking person, passionate and always eager to learn something new. I'm currently working in the service industry as a bartender, i have been doing it for over 5 years and i really enjoy it. I always make sure that i give the best service possible and ensure that all my guests are enjoying themselves and having a good time. As much as i enjoy bartending i've always been interested in programming and the tech industry as a whole. My goal is to become a full blown web developer and thanks to the MEDA program i was able to get my foot in the door and learn a few of the basics, the short experience i've had so far has been great and it's amazing to me how much more computers can do rather than perform simple tasks like surfing the web or playing games. I' m excited for what's to come and the challenges that lay ahead, i know transitioning into web developing won't be easy but it's a challenge that i'm willing to take on in order for me to succeed.
